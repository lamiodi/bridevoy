# BrideVoy Improvement Suggestions

## 1. Highest Priority Fixes

- Replace placeholder business values for WhatsApp, Instagram, and Formspree before launch.
- Fix navigation so every menu item points to a real section on the page.
- Align booking confirmation copy with the actual live workflow so the site does not overpromise invoice or payment automation.
- Update social preview metadata and assets so link sharing reflects the current brand correctly.

## 2. Section-by-Section Suggestions

### Hero
- Add a sharper supporting line under the main visual to explain the service in one sentence.
- Test the oversized BRIDEVOY background wordmark carefully on smaller phones to avoid visual crowding.
- Consider adding a short trust cue near the primary CTA, such as response speed or service area.

### Navbar
- Rename navigation items to existing section targets such as Home, About, Process, FAQ, and Book.
- Add WhatsApp as a direct mobile navigation action for faster inquiry flow.
- Keep the contact CTA visible, but make its destination and expectation clearer.

### About
- Bring the lower decorative service note more directly into the mobile content flow.
- Add at least one stronger trust proof such as completed bookings, planner partnerships, or repeat clients.
- Consider slightly tightening the copy for faster first-read comprehension.

### Why Choose Us
- Add simple icons or supporting visual markers to improve scanability.
- Consider one measurable proof point in at least one of the trust cards.
- Shorten feature descriptions slightly for better mobile readability.

### Mission and Vision
- Review the large-screen overlap spacing so the transition into the next section feels tighter.
- Consider a more brand-led heading that sounds less search-engine-driven in the visible UI.
- Ensure tablet layouts maintain strong rhythm without large empty vertical gaps.

### How It Works
- Add a short note explaining what happens after form submission.
- Include expected response timing if the business can commit to one.
- Consider a micro-summary at the end of the section to reinforce the ease of booking.

### FAQ
- Refine the heading and intro copy to feel more premium and on-brand.
- Add missing questions around payment timeline, service zones, availability hold, overtime, and standby.
- Keep answers concise and practical so the section remains easy to scan.

### Booking Wizard
- Prevent past dates directly in the date input, not only through validation messaging.
- Add a visible booking summary before final submission.
- Improve phone validation and consider address autocomplete in future versions.
- Replace tab semantics in the progress indicator unless each step becomes directly interactive.

### Footer and Contact
- Add real contact details such as WhatsApp, email, and phone if they are available.
- Include a final reassurance line about availability confirmation and reply timing.
- Keep the last CTA action-focused and consistent with the booking flow language.

## 3. Conversion Suggestions

- Add a lightweight pricing explanation block even if final quotes remain custom.
- Show sample Lagos service areas such as Ikoyi, Victoria Island, Lekki, and Ikeja GRA.
- Introduce testimonials, planner quotes, or visual social proof.
- Add a simple "What happens next" explanation near the booking section.

## 4. Responsive Design Suggestions

- Recheck the hero section and mission/vision transition on 320px to 375px screens.
- Reduce decorative spacing slightly on tablet widths to improve scroll rhythm.
- Make sure absolutely positioned decorative elements never feel detached on smaller screens.
- Keep CTA buttons easy to tap and consistently visible throughout mobile sections.
