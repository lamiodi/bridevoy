Privacy Policy

This page explains what information BrideVoy collects through this website, why we collect it, and how long we keep it. It applies only to bridevoy.com — not to any third-party service we link to.

What we collect

  • Details you submit on the booking form (name, email, WhatsApp number, event date, route, special requests).
  • Anonymous analytics (page views, device type) only if a Google Analytics ID has been configured for this domain.
  • One local-storage flag to remember that you have seen the preloader and any dismissals of the "Meet BrideVoy" intro card.

Why we collect it

Booking-form data is used solely to respond to your reservation request and to coordinate your rental. We do not sell, rent, or share your contact details with third parties for marketing.

How long we keep it

Form submissions are forwarded to our private email and Formspree inbox. Drafts of an in-progress booking are kept in your browser's session storage until you submit or close the tab. You can clear them at any time by clearing site data.

Your choices

You can ask us to delete your information at any time by emailing reservations@bridevoy.com. We respond to deletion requests within 7 business days.

Updates

We will update this page if our practices change. The "last updated" date below will always reflect the latest revision.
